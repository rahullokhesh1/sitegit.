Tricep Exercises

1.Close-Grip Bench Press:

Lie on a flat bench with your hands close together (about shoulder-width apart) on the barbell.
Lower the barbell to your chest and then push it back up to the starting position.
This exercise primarily targets the triceps.

2.Tricep Dips:

Use parallel bars or a sturdy surface like parallel handles on a dip station.
Lower your body by bending your elbows until your upper arms are parallel to the ground.
Push your body back up to the starting position.
This exercise effectively targets the triceps.

3.Tricep Pushdowns:

Use a cable machine with a straight or V-bar attachment.
Stand facing the machine with your elbows close to your sides.
Push the bar down while extending your elbows.
Return to the starting position.
This exercise isolates the triceps.

4.Skull Crushers (Lying Tricep Extensions):

Lie on a bench with a barbell or dumbbells in your hands.
Lower the weight by bending your elbows, bringing it close to your forehead.
Extend your arms to lift the weight back up.
This exercise targets the triceps.

5.Overhead Tricep Extension:

Hold a dumbbell or barbell overhead with both hands.
Bend your elbows to lower the weight behind your head.
Extend your arms to lift the weight back up.
This exercise works the triceps.

6.Diamond Push-Ups:

Get into a push-up position with your hands close together, forming a diamond shape with your thumbs and index fingers.
Lower your chest towards your hands and then push back up.
This variation of push-ups places extra emphasis on the triceps.

7.Tricep Kickbacks:

Hold a dumbbell in one hand and bend your knees slightly.
Bend forward at the waist and keep your back straight.
Extend your arm behind you, squeezing your triceps at the top.
This exercise isolates the triceps.

8.Close-Grip Push-Ups:

Perform a push-up with your hands close together, directly under your shoulders.
Lower your chest towards the ground and push back up.
This exercise targets the triceps.

9.Rope Tricep Pushdowns:

Use a rope attachment on a cable machine.
Hold the rope with both hands and push it down while extending your elbows.
Return to the starting position.
This exercise focuses on the triceps.

10.Bench Dips:

Sit on the edge of a bench with your hands gripping the edge.
Slide your buttocks off the bench and lower your body by bending your elbows.
Push your body back up to the starting position.
This exercise is effective for the triceps.
