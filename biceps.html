Biceps Exercises

1.Barbell Curls:

  • Hold a barbell with an underhand grip, hands shoulder-width apart.
  • Stand with your feet hip-width apart.
  • Keep your elbows close to your sides and curl the barbell toward your chest.
  • Lower it back down with control.
  • Perform 3 sets of 10-12 repetitions.

2.Dumbbell Curls:

  • Hold a dumbbell in each hand, palms facing forward.
  • Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart.
  • Curl the dumbbells upward while keeping your elbows stationary.
  • Lower the dumbbells back down slowly.
  • Perform 3 sets of 10-12 repetitions.

3.Hammer Curls:

  • Hold a dumbbell in each hand with your palms facing your torso.
  • Keep your arms at your sides and curl the dumbbells.
  • This exercise targets the brachialis muscle along with the biceps.
  • Perform 3 sets of 10-12 repetitions.

4.Preacher Curls:

  • Use a preacher curl bench with an EZ-curl bar or dumbbells.
  • Rest your arms on the preacher bench pad and curl the weight.
  • This exercise isolates the biceps effectively.
  • Perform 3 sets of 10-12 repetitions.

5.Concentration Curls:

  • Sit on a bench with your legs apart.
  • Rest your elbow against the inside of your thigh.
  • Curl a dumbbell using your biceps.
  • This exercise helps target the peak of the biceps.
  • Perform 3 sets of 10-12 repetitions for each arm.

6.Cable Curls:

  • Attach a straight or EZ-curl bar to a low pulley cable machine.
  • Stand in front of the cable machine, grip the bar, and curl it.
  • Maintain constant tension throughout the movement.
  • Perform 3 sets of 10-12 repetitions.

7.Chin-Ups:

  • Use a chin-up bar with an underhand grip (palms facing you).
  • Hang from the bar and pull your body upward until your chin clears the bar.
  • Lower your body back down.
  • Perform 3 sets of as many repetitions as possible.

8.Pull-Ups:

  • Use a pull-up bar with an overhand grip (palms facing away).
  • Hang from the bar and pull your body upward until your chin clears the bar.
  • Lower your body back down.
  • Perform 3 sets of as many repetitions as possible.
