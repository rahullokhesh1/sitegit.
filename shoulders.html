Shoulder Exercises

1.Shoulder Press (Overhead Press):

Sit or stand with proper posture, use a barbell, dumbbells, or a shoulder press machine, and press the weight overhead while engaging your core.

2.Lateral Raises:

Hold dumbbells by your sides, lift your arms outward to shoulder level, and maintain a slight elbow bend while controlling the motion.
This exercise effectively targets the triceps.

3.Front Raises:

Hold dumbbells in front of your thighs, lift your arms forward to shoulder level, and maintain a slight elbow bend while controlling the motion.

4.Rear Deltoid Flyes:

Alternate

Use a pec deck machine, bent-over dumbbell raises, or resistance bands to lift your arms backward, squeezing your shoulder blades together.

5.Face Pulls:

Attach a rope to a cable machine at chest height, pull the rope towards your face, and squeeze your shoulder blades together to engage the rear deltoids and trapezius.

6.Shrugs:

Hold dumbbells or use a barbell, and simply shrug your shoulders up toward your ears with a controlled range of motion to target the trapezius muscles.

7.Upright Rows:

Hold a barbell or EZ-curl bar with an overhand grip, lift it toward your chin while keeping it close to your body to work the lateral deltoids and trapezius muscles.

8.Arnold Press:

Press weights overhead while rotating your palms from facing you to facing forward to engage the entire shoulder complex.

9.High Pulls:

Use a barbell or dumbbells, performing an explosive movement targeting the anterior deltoids, similar to upright rows.

10.Push Press:

Employ leg drive to assist in pressing the weight overhead, engaging the entire shoulder complex in this compound exercise.
